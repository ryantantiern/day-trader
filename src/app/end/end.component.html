Debrief

This experiment compared the efficacy of traditional tools versus new technologies in trust development in virtual teams. Trust is a multi-facet construct which is thought to develop via interpersonal information exchange, risk and interdependence. As such, trust was measured via an intra-team trust survey and a prisoner’s dilemma-type game called Day Trader. We hope you have enjoyed this experiment.

Instructions

You are an investor for the next 5 days. Each day, you will receive $40 and have to decide how much you wish to invest as an individual and how much to invest with the group.

Rules

  1. The individual investment yields a guaranteed payoff of two times (2x) the amount invested per day. (eg. If individual investment = $20 then individual payoff = $20 X 2 = $40)
  2. In the group investment, all playersâ€™ contributions are lumped together, and multiplied by 4, then distributed back evenly.
  3. For example, Player 1 invests, $40 individually and $0 group investment, Player 2 invests, $30 individually and $10 group investment, Player 3 invests, $35 individually and $5 group investment and Player 4 invests $20 individually and $20 group investment.
  4. Final return on group investment per person is:
    1. (($0 + $10 + $5 + $20) x 4) / 4 = $35
    2. Therefore, each individual receives $35 return on group investment
